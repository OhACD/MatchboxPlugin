# FAQ & Troubleshooting

Q: Spawns/seats not found after restart?
A: Check world folder names; `/matchbox listspawns` marks missing worlds.

Q: Permissions not working?
A: Verify permission nodes and your server's permission plugin.

Q: Player skins inconsistent?
A: Check `cosmetics.use-steve-skins` and `cosmetics.random-skins-enabled` config flags.

If unsure, provide server logs and ask in Discord (https://discord.gg/BTDP3APfq8).
